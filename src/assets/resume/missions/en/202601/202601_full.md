- The project involved creating a resilient registry system tailored for MCP servers, addressing the intricate governance requirements of Air France KLM.
  - Fine-grained permission management was implemented to safeguard data flows with precise security measures.
  - Careful attention was paid to harmonizing the solution with the technical and organizational particularities of the AFKLM ecosystem.
- Collaboration within a cross-functional team of four developers, one Product Owner, and one Business Analyst ensured alignment of functional and technical objectives.
  - Agile methodologies fostered continuous adaptation to evolving business and technical needs.
- The strategy hinged on **infrastructure as code**, enabling streamlined automation of deployments and effective governance over configurations.
  - Leveraging modern technologies guaranteed a scalable and maintainable solution over time.

**Technologies used:**
```text
Java 21, Camunda, Angular, SpringBoot, Spring Data, Github Actions, Ansible, AKS
```
