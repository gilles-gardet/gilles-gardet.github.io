Implementation of an MCP server registry within Air France KLM to ensure **governance** and **control** over distributed services designed for AI agents. The project supports technological innovation by structuring and regulating service exchanges.  
**Main Features:** centralized MCP server management, access and availability monitoring, user-friendly interface.  
**Team:** 5 developers, 1 architect, 1 product owner, 1 business analyst.  
**Methodology:** agile Scrum based, promoting collaboration and rapid iterations.
