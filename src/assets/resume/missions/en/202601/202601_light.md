Implementation of an MCP server registry at Air France KLM to provide governance and control over AI agents' service and data provisioning. The project standardizes and secures resource availability through a centralized solution.  
**Context:** The development addresses the increasing need for integration and oversight of MCP servers in a complex multi-stakeholder environment.  
**Main Features:** centralized server management, access control, usage monitoring, reporting.  
**Team:** 5 developers, 1 architect, 1 product owner, 1 business analyst.  
**Methodology:** Agile based on Scrum with close team collaboration.
