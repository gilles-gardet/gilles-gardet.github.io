Implementation of an MCP server registry within Air France KLM, aimed at establishing **strict governance** and control over service and data availability for AI agents. The mission includes the full **design** and **implementation** of the solution.  
This registry provides centralized and secure data access, facilitating controlled use by AI agents throughout the company.  
**Main Features:** MCP server management, access tracking, service catalog, data control and auditing.  
**Team:** 5 developers, 1 architect, 1 product owner, 1 business analyst.  
**Methodology:** Agile approach based on Scrum with regular sprints and iterative reviews.
