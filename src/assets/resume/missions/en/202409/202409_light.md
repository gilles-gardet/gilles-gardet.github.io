**Mission Objective:**  
Develop a modern, scalable **e-commerce platform** for Acme Corp, enhancing user experience and operational efficiency. Implement robust microservices to streamline product catalog, order processing, and payment management.

**Context:**  
Acme Corp required a **cloud-native solution** to support growth in retail while ensuring secure transactions and real-time inventory updates. The platform needed to be scalable, maintainable, and responsive to fast-changing market demands.

**Main Features:**  
- Microservices for product catalog, order, and payment management  
- Secure payment integration  
- Real-time inventory management system  
- Cloud-native deployment using Kubernetes  

**Team:**  
Cross-functional agile team of **5 developers, 1 product owner, and 1 scrum master**

**Methodology:**  
Agile approach with **2-week sprint cycles** fostering continuous integration and delivery
