**Mission Summary**

Developed a **modern e-commerce platform** for Acme Corp to streamline retail operations and enhance customer experience. Delivered scalable microservices managing product catalogs, orders, and payments with a focus on **security and real-time inventory management**.

The project addressed the need for a robust, cloud-native solution capable of handling high transaction volumes and dynamic inventory updates, ensuring seamless and secure customer interactions.

**Main Features:**
- Microservices for product catalog, order processing, and payment handling  
- Real-time inventory management system  
- Secure payment integration  
- Cloud-native deployment with Kubernetes  

**Team:**  
Cross-functional agile team of 6 developers, including backend, frontend, and DevOps specialists

**Methodology:**  
Agile with 2-week sprints, emphasizing continuous delivery and iterative improvements
