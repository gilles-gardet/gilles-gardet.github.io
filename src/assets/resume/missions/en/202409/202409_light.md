**Mission Summary**

Development of a modern **e-commerce platform** for Acme Corp to enhance retail operations through scalable and secure digital solutions. The project focuses on designing microservices for product catalog, order management, and payments, deployed on a resilient cloud-native infrastructure.

The client required a robust platform to support growing online sales, streamline inventory management in real time, and ensure secure payment processing while maintaining high availability and scalability.

**Main Features:**  
- Microservices architecture for catalog, orders, and payment processing  
- Real-time inventory management integration  
- Secure payment gateway implementation  
- Cloud-native deployment with orchestration  
- Responsive front-end for seamless user experience  

**Team:**  
Cross-functional agile team including backend and frontend developers, DevOps engineers, and QA specialists.

**Methodology:**  
Agile framework with 2-week sprints fostering continuous delivery and rapid feedback.
