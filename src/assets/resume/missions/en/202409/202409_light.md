**Mission Summary**

The objective was to develop a **modern e-commerce platform** for Acme Corp, enhancing retail operations with scalable, cloud-native solutions. The platform supports seamless product management, order processing, and secure payments to improve user experience and operational efficiency.

Acme Corp required a robust system capable of handling increasing traffic and complex workflows while maintaining real-time inventory accuracy and payment security. The challenge was to design a flexible, microservice-based architecture deployable on Kubernetes for scalability and resilience.

**Main Features:**  
- Microservices for product catalog, orders, and payments  
- Real-time inventory management system  
- Integration of secure payment gateways  
- Cloud-native deployment with Kubernetes

**Team:**  
- Cross-functional agile team with developers, QA, and DevOps specialists

**Methodology:**  
- Agile approach with 2-week sprints, continuous integration, and iterative delivery
