**Mission Objective:**  
Develop a **modern e-commerce platform** for Acme Corp to enhance retail operations through scalable and secure microservices. Deliver a cloud-native solution ensuring seamless order processing, payment integration, and inventory management.

**Context:**  
Acme Corp required a robust platform to replace legacy systems, addressing challenges in real-time inventory tracking, secure payment processing, and scalable product catalog management to support business growth.

**Main Features:**  
- Microservices for product catalog, orders, and payments  
- Secure payment gateway integration  
- Real-time inventory management system  
- Cloud-native deployment on Kubernetes  

**Team:**  
Cross-functional agile team including backend and frontend developers, DevOps engineers, and product owners.

**Methodology:**  
Agile development with 2-week sprints, emphasizing continuous delivery and iterative improvements.
