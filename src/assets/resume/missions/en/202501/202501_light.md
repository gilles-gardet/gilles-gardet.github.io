KAAS (KPI as a Service) goal is the implementation of KPIs on a REST API for Air France KLM, covering business areas such as oil prices, ground staff performance, and delays.

The project relies on an ETL system orchestrated by Control-M that retrieves and transforms data from Teradata, temporarily storing it in PostgreSQL.

- Main Features: Data extraction, transformation and loading, real-time data collection, HiveMQ-based dissemination, customizable dashboards available on the UI.  
- 3 developers, 1 product owner.  
- Methodology Kanban with continuous task tracking and dynamic prioritization.
