Implementation of KPIs on a REST API for Air France KLM, covering business areas such as oil prices, ground staff performance, and delays. The project relies on an ETL system orchestrated by Control-M that retrieves and transforms data from Teradata, temporarily storing it in PostgreSQL.  
**Main Features:** Data extraction, transformation and loading, real-time data collection, HiveMQ-based dissemination, customizable dashboards available on the UI.  
**Team:** 3 developers, 1 product owner.  
**Methodology:** Kanban with continuous task tracking and dynamic prioritization.
